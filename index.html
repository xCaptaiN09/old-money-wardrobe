VII Colours to Avoid

These tones are incompatible with the aesthetic — not because of personal preference, but because they break the natural, muted logic the entire palette is built on.

Black
Too stark, demands attention, zero warmth
Bright White
Clinical and cold — use cream or ivory
Bright Blue
Reads sporty — use navy only
Red
Too loud, no natural grounding
Purple
No relationship to natural materials
Bright Orange
Streetwear register — use terracotta
Neon / Lime
Antithetical to every principle here
Bright Green
Keep all greens dark and muted
Pink / Blush
No heritage grounding in men's old money
Steel Blue / Teal
Too modern and corporate in tone
Silver / Light Grey
Use warm grey — pure silver reads synthetic
Bright Yellow
Use muted mustard only — not bright
